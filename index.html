Accessibility

(a11y)

Who are these people?

  • People with visual impairments
  • People with hearing impairments
  • People with mobility impairments
  • People with cognitive impairments

Ways of interaction

Blind people

  • Screen readers
  • Accessibility from the keyboard
  • Semantic markup

People with visually impaired

  • Font size
  • Notable links
  • Contrast
  • Text explanations
  • outline:none
  • Adaptability

People with hearing impairments

  • subtitles
  • method of communication

People with motoric problem

  • Navigation with keyboard
  • Large clickable areas

People with cognitive impairments

  • Delivering content in more than one way
  • Easily understood content
  • Focusing attention on important content
  • Minimizing distractions
  • Consistent webpage layout and navigation
  • Familiar elements
  • Dividing processes
  • Website authentication is as easy as possible
  • Making forms easy to complete

Accessibility guidelines and the law

  • Web content availability guide (WCAG 2.0)
  • GOST R 52872-2012 "Internet resources. Accessibility requirements for disabled victims"
